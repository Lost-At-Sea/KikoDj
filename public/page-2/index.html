Bio

K!KO is a progressive house and trance producer who is currently a resident DJ at Treehouse Miami. He's headlined events such as Festidan, played events at Grand Central and the Groove Cruise, and has quickly become known as the go-to party starter of Miami, Florida. Follow his social media to catch his KIKBack podcast on Soundcloud, his live sets on Twitch, and his next set near you!
